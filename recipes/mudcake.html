Mudcake

Description

I will not bore you with a long story of my family history.

Ingredients

  • 100g butter
  • 2.5dl caster sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1dl wheat flour
  • 3tbls cacao
  • 1tsp vanilla sugar
  • Powdered sugar (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Set the oven to 170 degrees Celsius
  2. Melt the butter in a pot, lift the pot off the heat
  3. Stir down the butter and eggs, mix well. Add the remaining ingredients and make sure it is well combined.
  4. Pour the batter in a buttered and breaded tin with a removable edge, about 24cm in diameter
  5. Bake in the middle of the oven for about 15 minutes. The cake should be low with a fairly hard surface and slightly sticky in the middle.
  6. Let the cake cool. Garnish with a powdering of powdered sugar. Serve with cream or ice-cream and fruit
  7. ENJOY!
